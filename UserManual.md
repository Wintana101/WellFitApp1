 **User Manual – Well-Fit App 1** 



**Getting Started** 



Open the app 



Sign up with your name, email, and password 



Log in to access the dashboard 



**Home Screen** 



View a personalized greeting 



Enter daily steps 



Track progress toward daily step goal 



View rotating motivational messages 



Track daily water intake 



**Workout Screen** 



Displays personalized workouts 



Workouts change daily and differ per user 



Each workout shows duration 



**Progress Screen** 



View weekly activity summary 



Track completed workouts and steps 



**Profile Screen** 



View name and email 



Logout securely 



**Logout** 



-Tap “Logout” from the Profile screen to securely exit the app.
